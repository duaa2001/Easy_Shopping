BIPOC:

Our site aims to promote color-owned businesses. If you are asking why should you shop from these businesses, you have come to the right place.If we support color-owned businesses, we increase diversity and representation in our economy. We will be closer to closing the racial wealth gap. On top of that, many of these businesses lead to more jobs for colored peoples. If that weren't enough, we aim to celebrate all cultures and achieve a sense of pride as a result of more inclusivity. At "Unbrand the Brands," We strive to better our community.
